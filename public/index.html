Scan. Order. Eat.

A QR-code based food ordering system — no queues, no waiting for waiters, just scan and order.

Register Your Restaurant

📱 Scan & Order

Customers scan the QR code on their table, browse the live menu, and place orders directly from their phone.

🧑‍🍳 Live Order Status

Track orders in real time: Placed → Preparing → Ready → Delivered, no need to flag down a waiter.

🛠️ Admin Dashboard

Restaurant owners can add/edit/delete menu items with photos and prices, and manage incoming orders.

💳 Easy Payments

Customers can pay directly from their phone after ordering — no waiting for the bill.

📝 Special Requests

Customers can add comments or special instructions with every order (e.g. "less spicy", "no onions").

🔗 Unique QR per Shop

Every restaurant gets its own unique QR code linking straight to its digital menu.
